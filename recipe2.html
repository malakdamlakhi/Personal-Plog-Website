Cubcake

the components

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • Oil for frying

Preparation method

1.Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add the warm milk,
2. melted butter, and egg. Knead the ingredients until you get a smooth and cohesive dough.
3.Let the Dough Rise: Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour,
4.until it doubles in size.
3. Shape the Donuts: Once the dough has risen, roll it out to about 1 cm thickness.
5. Use a round cutter to make donut shapes, with a smaller hole in the center.

9.or drizzle with melted chocolate as you like.
10. Enjoy!
